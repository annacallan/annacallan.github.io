Anna Callan

I am a research scientist with a PhD in Biochemistry and over 10 years experience in research in environmental epidemiology, with a focus on prenatal exposures and child health. I am also an experienced lecturer/public speaker and have taught in undergraduate and postgraduate programs, as well as presenting my work at national and international conferences. I have experience in building databases for research data and in applying statistical methods for data analysis. In 2018 I began studying an IBM Certificate in Data Science, which included using Python for data visualisation and analysis, as well as machine learning. In 2019 I am very proud to be one of the first 25 women to take part in She Codes Australia Plus, an exciting program powered by BHP and Spacecubed. I am passionate about encouraging women to pursue careers in STEM. I am also a bit of a crazy cat lady on the side.
